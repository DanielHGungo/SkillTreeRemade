Puppetteere

You can invoke your spells to originate from an undead you control and can see.

Reaper's Blade

You may pick one of these damage types: piercing/bludgeoning/slashing/force. When you deal damage with the chosen type, you can choose to change it to necrotic instead.

Adept Necromancer

When you cast a necromancy spell 3rd level or lower, you may roll a d100. On a 50 or higher you don't lose the spell slot.

Loyal Skelly

At the beginning of combat if you have no undead, you may have a skeleton archer conjure beside you.

Shared Tether

As an action you may transfer hp from one undead you control or yourself, into yourself or another undead you control.

All Rise!

Once per day as an action, you can revive any undead within 30feet of you with 10 hitpoints each.

Death's Tongue

You can cast speak with dead at will.

Protect the Lich!

Once per day as a bonus action, you can give an undead you control an extra turn after yours.

Siphon Life

Once per day you can deal 100 force damage to an undead as an action. If it dies from this ability, you gain 10d6 temporary hp.
